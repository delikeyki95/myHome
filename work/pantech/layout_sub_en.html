PANTECH led to changes in the world market

CEO

Let us introduce the CEO of Pantech,
a global leading company
in the Intelligent Mobile Device industry

Since its founding in 1991, Pantech has achieved remarkable growth by highly improving convenience in our day to day lives.
Thanks to their state-of-the-art technologies in the IT industry, Pantech has been enjoying their status as a telecommunications leader over the past 20 years.


Meanwhile, Pantech has had to face a series of crises due to rapidly evolving technologies and a paradigm shift in the telecommunication industry; however, Pantech’s constant innovation and challenging spirit have enabled them to find new opportunities in such times of crisis.


Since Pantech was always one step ahead of their competitors in the new smartphone competition, their innovative products have enabled Pantech to become firmly positioned as a premium class smartphone manufacturer. In addition, Pantech has strategically released differentiated products in overseas markets such North America and Japan in order to strengthen their market share and penetrate into other markets like Europe and China.
Pantech’s achievements and technical progress however, would not have been possible had there not been loyalty from their customers.

In return for all the attention we have received, we, the executives and staff members, will come up with a practical alternative which will be suitable for the new market environment through continuous training and research. By nurturing our strengths, including our challenging spirit, our innovation skills, good judgment and new ideas for finding capabilities, we will dedicate our best efforts in making Pantech a long-living corporation.


We will prove that Pantech can channel its potential into mainstream success
as a global IMD enterprise and provide what customers need most in the future.
Thank you

Chung Joon, CEO of Pantech
Moon Ji-wook, President of Pantech
