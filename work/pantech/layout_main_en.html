Business Network

Pantech established and new challenges started.

  • Intelligent Mobile Device

    Since it introduced the Vega LTE, the world’s first motion recognition smartphone at the dawn of the LTE era, Pantech has been continuing to solidify its position in the domestic smartphone market by introducing the Vega Iron, with the world’s first seamless metal design and the IM-100 with its precision operable wheel key.

  • Smart Wearable Device

    Utilizing its cutting-edge technology accumulated through their manufacture of various premium smartphones, Pantech is focusing on securing new technologies to diversify its product portfolio towards wearable devices such as smartwatches that are closely related to the Internet of Things or IoT.

  • Advanced IoT Device

    Pantech is currently developing and commercializing devices that conform to IoT standards, based on the LTE based M2M modules and routers and is now preparing to expand their offering to IoT related services.
